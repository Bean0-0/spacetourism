IT STARTS WITH YOU.|

No longer is space travel limited to heavily funded government expeditions. The Armstrong 24-month Space Program provides you with the knowledge and resources needed to shape you into a fully fledged astronaut. Our program covers all areas of space travel, from on-ground planning and preparation to in-space exercise and nutrition. Those who finish the program are granted with an official NASA Astronaut Certificate and an all-expenses paid 3 month voyage into deep space.
